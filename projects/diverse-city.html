Simple City Builder (Nov-2020 - Present)

Simple City Builder is a game that is under development in Unity. It originally was developed for a week-long college-hosted game jam with the theme of 'diversity' and under the name of 'diverse-city'. The original idea of this game is that you create a city that aims to attract and encourage diversity of the different types of population as shapes. The art style of the game is intended to be very simple and is drawn using Inkscape.

The aim of the game was to create the largest population as you can. You will be limited by money and need to be profitable, and different shapes have different needs and interests which should be considered when designing you city.

The new idea for this game is more alike other city builders but I intend to simplify some elements to it, currently. You aim to make a successful city where success is determined by several factors: Money, Niceness and others. I decided to change the project to something that is more interesting and enjoyable for me to make and one that I have more ideas for.

To follow progress of the game visit Simple City Builder project board
