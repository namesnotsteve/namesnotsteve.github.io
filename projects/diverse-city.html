Diverse City (Nov-2020)

Diverse city is a game that is under development in Unity. It originally was developed for a week-long college-hosted game jam with the theme of 'diversity'. The idea of this game is that you create a city that aims to attract and encourage diversity of the different types of population as shapes. The art style of the game is intended to be very simple and is drawn using Inkscape.

The aim of the game is to create the largest population as you can. You will be limited by money and need to be profitable, and different shapes have different needs and interests which should be considered when designing you city.

To follow progress of the game visit Diverse City project board
